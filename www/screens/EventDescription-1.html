TOFINO SURF EVENTS 2020

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

Time & Date

March 4-6

Price

Free

Location

Tofino, British Columbia

Canada's female-friendly surf competition is back in 2020 with the 11th annual Queen of the Peak in Tofino. This Surf contest was created to showcase and celebrate the amazing female Tofino Surfing talent we have on the west coast.

The Queen of the Peak is presented by Surf Sister, Long Beach Surf Shop, Pacific Sands Beach Resort, Shelter Restaurant, and Billabong. Competitors and Spectators are asked to bike or carpool to all Tofino Surf Events. Proceeds from this Tofino Surf Event are donated to Keep a Breast Canada.
