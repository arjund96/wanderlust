FAQs

ANY QUESTIONS ?

What exactly is WanderLust ?

Wanderlust is a perfect mobile application for discovering new and exciting leisure activities! Be the first to get filtered recommendations for travel destinations, live events, restaurants and more, according to your preferences with just one click. This application is also a great way to find your way to the desired event’s location.

Is Wanderlust free?

Yes, it’s totally free and there is no in-app purchase! You can just download the application and enjoy surfing through your list of recommendations.

What kind of activity suggestions can I get on Wanderlust?

The sky's the limit! With Wanderlust, you can get the recommendations for travel destinations, live events, sport competitions, concerts, restaurants and even a Baby Shark live performance! Anything you desire, we have it.

How can I use Wanderlust?

Wanderlust is as simple as it can be! When signing up, you can freely select your preferences for different categories. Wanderlust then gets you covered by saving your choices to suggest the best matches for you. You can simply browse through the list and personalize the recommendations according to how you like it to be.

Can I change my preferences on Wanderlust?

Yes, it’s a piece of cake! You can change your personal preferences by following these simple steps:
1) Select Setting > Preference Setting.
2) Add or remove the options according to your liking.
3) Press “Save”.
4) Enjoy a new list of suggestions!

How can I contact Wanderlust for business inquiries?

If you have any question, comment, or want us to feature your business on Wanderlust, you can easily reach out to us by following with these steps:
1) Select Setting > Contact Us.
2) Fill in the required fields.
3) Click “Send”.
4) Reply to our email with the information requested.
5) Allow 2-4 business days for us to follow up.
